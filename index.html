Where We Shoot

Our Favorite Oahu Spots

We know the island's best-kept secrets for golden light and dreamy backdrops.

North Shore

Wild waves, golden sand, and the most epic sunsets on the island.

Kailua Beach

Soft turquoise water and powder-white sand. A family session dream.

Diamond Head

Dramatic cliffs and lush green with Waikiki sparkling in the distance.

Lanikai

Sunrise views with the Mokulua Islands framing every shot.

Waimanalo

Lush ironwood trees and untouched shoreline. Quiet, beautiful, perfect.

Ko Olina

Calm lagoons with resort-style elegance. Ideal for couples and proposals.

Hey there! We're Kelsey and Chase — the husband-and-wife duo behind LuLuLens. We fell in love with Oahu (and each other) and decided to turn our passion for photography into something special.

Our style is warm, natural, and full of island energy. We love chasing golden hour, playing in the waves, and capturing those in-between moments that make your heart melt.

Whether you're visiting Oahu for vacation, celebrating a milestone, or just want beautiful photos with your people — we've got you.
